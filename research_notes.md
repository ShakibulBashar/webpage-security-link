# Research Notes

## Services document

### Guard Division
- Guard division is the core service.
- Emphasis is on slow, systematic growth rather than rapid expansion.
- Quality is defined through character, behavior, and competence.
- Field supervision uses at least two layers of hierarchy at ground level.
- HQ arranges daily, weekly, and monthly supervision.
- Personnel quality is maintained through selection, training, assessment, and supervision.
- There is a regular assessment and promotion system every six months.
- Police verification, documentation, and identity checks are used for background verification.
- Employee motivation is supported through salary, promotion, fair assessment, and management access.

### Logistics Division
- Division is being developed gradually to support clients.
- Mentioned service areas: cleaning, lift servicing, power sub-station servicing, bill deposition.
- Cleaning categories listed: industrial cleaning, hospital cleaning, corporate house/office cleaning.

### Risk Management Division
- Focuses on risk assessment, physical security design, security technology planning, and executive protection.
- Emphasizes that each asset has a different threat profile based on area, exposure, and guardianship.
- Encourages thorough risk assessment and integrated security design using people, process, technology, and awareness.
- Periodic surveys and audits are considered mandatory.
- SecurityLink has experienced consultants with over 30 years in the field.
